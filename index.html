About Me

Hello! My name is Brandy Nogales, and I am currently pursuing an MSc in Cyber Security at ETHZ-EPFL, Zurich. I hold a BSc in Computer Sciences and a BSc in Psychology from the University of Geneva. My expertise includes a range of programming languages such as Python, C, C++, Java, JavaScript, and Scala. I have substantial experience in software engineering, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence. In addition to my technical skills, I am proficient in English, French, and Spanish.

During my academic and professional career, I have led software engineering projects, developed full-stack applications, and conducted research in the field of emotionally intelligent peripherals for video games. My recent work at ETHZ focused on exploiting system vulnerabilities and machine learning attacks. I am passionate about literature, astronomy, photography, and boxing.
